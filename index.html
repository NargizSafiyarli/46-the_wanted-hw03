About us

The Wanted, also known as Team 46, is a student group from ADA University, consisting of Nargiz Safiyarli, Rafael Heybatzada, and Bahman Sariyev. The team has already successfully completed projects like the "Hour of Code" event, aimed at introducing programming to schoolchildren, and the "How Websites Work" assignment, which involved creating an introductory website about the team.
